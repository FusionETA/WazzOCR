Best Practices
Tips for Better Results
☀️

Good Lighting

Images with even, bright lighting produce the most accurate text extraction.

📐

Keep It Straight

Skewed or rotated text reduces accuracy. Try to keep text aligned horizontally.

🔎

Higher Resolution

Larger, clearer images give better results. Avoid blurry or compressed photos.

🖨️

Printed Text Works Best

Typed/printed text extracts almost perfectly. Handwriting accuracy varies.
