Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

WazzOCR is a service operated by FusionETA Sdn Bhd ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use WazzOCR, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using WazzOCR you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

FusionETA Sdn Bhd is a technology company registered in Malaysia. WazzOCR is our bill and receipt automation product that processes images sent via WhatsApp and creates draft bills in Xero on your behalf.

Contact: hello@fusioneta.com.my

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash) when you register.
  • WhatsApp messages: images and documents you or your team send to our shared or your own WhatsApp channel. We process these solely to extract bill data.
  • Bill and receipt data: supplier names, invoice numbers, amounts, dates, and line items extracted from your documents via AI OCR.
  • Xero connection: OAuth 2.0 tokens that allow us to create draft bills in your Xero organisation on your behalf. We do not store your Xero login credentials.
  • Phone numbers: WhatsApp sender numbers you add to your allowed-numbers list for access control.
  • Usage logs: IP addresses, browser/device information, and timestamps for security and troubleshooting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core WazzOCR service — extracting bill data from images and creating Xero draft bills.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To send transactional emails (account verification, password reset). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To improve and debug the service using anonymised usage data.

4. Xero Integration

When you connect Xero, we request only the scopes needed to create and read draft bills. We store your Xero OAuth tokens securely and use them only to perform actions you have authorised. You can disconnect Xero at any time from your account settings, which revokes our access.

5. WhatsApp & Wazzup

WazzOCR integrates with the Wazzup platform to receive WhatsApp messages. Images sent to your channel are processed in real time and are not permanently stored beyond what is needed to create your bill records. Wazzup's own privacy policy applies to data processed through their platform.

6. AI Processing

Bill images are sent to a third-party AI vision service (Google Gemini) for OCR and data extraction. Images are processed transiently and are not used to train AI models. Google's data processing terms apply.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Xero — to create bills in your organisation.
  • Wazzup — to receive WhatsApp messages via their API.
  • Google (Gemini API) — for AI OCR processing.
  • Infrastructure providers — cloud hosting (DigitalOcean) and database services, under strict data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement — if required by applicable law.

8. Data Retention

Bill records are retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete individual bill records from your dashboard. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data: HTTPS in transit, encrypted storage for sensitive credentials, hashed passwords, and access controls limiting who can view your data. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@fusioneta.com.my. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Cookies

WazzOCR uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

12. Children

WazzOCR is a business tool intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
